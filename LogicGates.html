Logic Gates & Truth Table Fundamentals

Logic Gates: Circuits that perform logical operations on binary inputs to produce an output.

Truth Table: Shows all possible combinations of inputs and corresponding outputs.

Representation: HIGH (1) = +5V, LOW (0) = 0V.
